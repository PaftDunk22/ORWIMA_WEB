Runick is an archetype whose gameplan is based on controlling the flow of the game with Spell Cards, while banishing cards from the opponents deck and forgoing their own Battle Phase.
The archetype maintans card advantage via their Field Spell "Runick Fountain", allowing the deck to constantly replenish the hand with new Spells.
This flexibility allows the Deck to be paired with a lot of other Decks, allowing for so-called "Runick Variants"
